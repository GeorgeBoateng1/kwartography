How a Shoot Works

01
Inquiry & Consultation
Tell me about your shoot — the occasion, the feeling you want, the location you have in mind. We'll talk it through and make sure we're a great fit.
02
Pre-Shoot Planning
Location scouting, outfit guidance, timing for the best light, and a clear shot list so we both arrive fully prepared.
03
The Session
A relaxed shoot where I direct naturally and capture real moments — no stiff poses, just you at your best in front of the camera.
04
Your Gallery
Professionally retouched images delivered to a private online gallery within the agreed turnaround. High-res downloads and print orders included.
